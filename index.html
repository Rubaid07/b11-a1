The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 represents a new era of transformation, focusing on technological advancement, education, and economic growth. With the vision of building a "Smart Bangladesh," this concept emphasizes sustainable development, innovation, and improving the quality of life for its citizens. Key pillars of this vision include expanding digitalization, fostering a thriving startup culture, and enhancing global competitiveness. By leveraging the creativity and entrepreneurial spirit of its youth, Bangladesh 2.0 aspires to become a global model of progress. This vision is not limited to economic growth alone but also aims for inclusive development across all sectors. From rural areas to urban hubs, the goal is to provide modern services and opportunities to every corner of the nation, creating a smarter, more connected Bangladesh."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
